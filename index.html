Frequently asked questions

My design process typically involves four key phases: research, design, prototype, and test. In the research phase, I gather insights about the user and their needs. In the design phase, I create wireframes and visual designs that meet those needs. In the prototype phase, I create interactive models of the design for testing. In the test phase, I collect feedback from users to refine the design.

I use a variety of industry-standard tools including Figma for design and prototyping, Adobe Creative Suite for graphics, Webflow for development, and various user research tools like Hotjar and Google Analytics for insights.

Success is measured through various metrics including user engagement rates, conversion improvements, task completion rates, user satisfaction scores, and business KPIs. I also conduct usability testing and gather qualitative feedback to ensure the design meets user needs.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website redesign takes 4-8 weeks, while a complete brand identity project might take 6-12 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.
